## Overview  

**Sound Forge for Mac** is a complete audio editing and production tool designed for musicians, producers, and audio engineers. It provides a wide range of features for recording, editing, mixing, and mastering audio tracks with professional precision.  

The software allows high-resolution audio editing up to 32-bit/384kHz and supports multi-channel recording. Users can clean up audio recordings with noise reduction tools, remove clicks and pops, and apply equalization, compression, and effects for polished results. Sound Forge also offers batch processing, real-time monitoring, and plugin support for VST and AU effects.  

Ideal for both creative professionals and advanced hobbyists, Sound Forge provides a versatile environment for producing high-quality sound projects. Optimized for Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, the app delivers reliable performance without compromising system speed, making it a trusted choice for professional audio production workflows.
